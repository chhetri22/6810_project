We built a product that actuates as you move

If you have too much weight on your right foot, the servo actuates to tighten your shoelaces, helping you to intuitively move back to symmetric standing. Note how the height of each shoe in the visualization corresponds to the weight that is exerted on that foot. Note that the servo only actuates when the weight is uneven toward the right foot.
