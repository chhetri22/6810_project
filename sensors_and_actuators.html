Sensor

We use four Force Sensitive Resistors (FSR's) attached to the bottom of the shoe's sole to accurately approximate the weight with minimal discomfort to the user. All four FSR's are connected to an Arduino Nano.

Actuator

When the device notices an imbalance, a servo motor pulls the laces so that the user can have an unintrusive experience while being aware of the imbalance. The servo receives power and input from the 5V output of the microcontroller (Arduino Nano)
